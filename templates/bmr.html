What is BMR ?

The Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) Calculator determines the number of calories your body needs to maintain essential functions while at complete rest. This measurement is taken in a stable environment with a neutral temperature and after a period of fasting (usually about 12 hours), when the digestive system is inactive.
