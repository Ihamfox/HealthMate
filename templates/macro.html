Macronutrients (Macros) :

Macronutrients are the main nutrients that provide energy in large amounts: carbohydrates, proteins, and fats. This calculator focuses on daily needs for carbs, protein, and fat.

  • Proteins: Made of amino acids, essential for health. Sources include meat, dairy, beans, and nuts. Choose healthier options like fish and lean meats over processed ones.
  • Carbohydrates: Include sugars, starches, and fibers. Simple carbs (sugars) and complex carbs (fiber-rich foods like vegetables and whole grains) affect energy and health differently.
  • Fats: Provide energy and have various types, including saturated, unsaturated, and omega-3 fats. Aim to limit saturated and trans fats, and focus on healthier fats like monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats.
